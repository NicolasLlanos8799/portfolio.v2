About Me

I started my career participating in the development and evolution of digital products within technical teams, working on architecture, business logic, and systems building.

From the beginning, I was interested in understanding not just the code, but the comprehensive functioning of the product.

That stage allowed me to learn not only on a technical level but also on a human and organizational one. I understood how a company works from the inside: teamwork, communication between departments, planning, and shared responsibility.

Over time, that vision transformed into a clear motivation: building end-to-end solutions.

Not just developing features, but designing the structure, defining the logic, integrating technologies, and taking an idea from conception to actual implementation.

Working more independently confirmed that what truly drives me is the complete creation process. I am excited to imagine a solution, structure it, and see it work in the real world.

I enjoy interacting with clients, understanding their needs, and translating them into clear, well-thought-out systems.

I am not defined by a single technology; I am defined by the ability to build and integrate systems with a product vision.
